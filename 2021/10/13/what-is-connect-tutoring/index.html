What is Connect Tutoring?

Posted on October 13, 2021October 14, 2021 by Connect Tutoring

Connect Tutoring is a volunteer program to provide free online tutoring to K-12 students in Calgary and Edmonton. We provide tutoring to students that cannot afford or do not have access to academic support.

Connect Tutoring was founded in November 2020 amidst the difficulties of the COVID-19 pandemic. To learn more about our program and how it works, please see our program page.

Connect Tutoring – Edmonton

Great news! Connect Tutoring recently expanded to add a team in Edmonton, Alberta. Connect Tutoring Edmonton is run by a team of university students and will be offering support to students in Edmonton.

To learn more about the team, please check out our team page.
